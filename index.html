About

I am a Bachelor of Computer Engineering student at Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU). Throughout my time at TMU, I have completed numerous projects, including a music hosting website and a maze guidance system for an HCS12 microcontroller robot. These experiences have shaped me into a person who embraces challenges and constantly seeks growth.

I am passionate about programming and developing innovative software. I have a strong desire to learn and continuously improve my skills. My goal for the future is to become a full stack developer with expertise in various programming languages. I aspire to use these skills to develop impactful software that can make a positive difference in the world.
